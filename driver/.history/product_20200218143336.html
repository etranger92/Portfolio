MONT ST MICHEL

Excursions
  • DESCRIPTION
  • INFORMATIONS

Mont St Michel is a place of sheer enchantment in the middle of the bay. Marvel of the western world, the granite island almost 80 meters high forms a picturesque and monumental sight. Ringed by treacherous quick sands and accessible only by a seawall. Mont St Michel is the symbol of Normandy and a Unesco World Heritage site.

Pick up at your hotel at 7.00 am you will arrive around 11.00 am.

The tour cross the Normandy countryside called “le Bocage Normand” which is one of the most beautiful in France with its small typical villages, farms and cattle which are producing the famous Camembert, Livarot and Pont l’Eveque cheeses.

You will have plenty of time to visit the site, its monumental halls, the cloister, the state chambers called “ La Merveille” and the lace staircase. You will stroll on the covered way of the ramparts and inside the village’s alleys. Lunch on your own. There are many nice small restaurants in the village of which the famous “Mère Poulard” restaurant and shops.

You will leave the Mont St Michel around 4.00/4.30 pm. Return to Paris around 8.30 pm.
