About

Kids

I worked almost 10 years among them, mainly as a social worker and three years as a teacher.

It was essential for me to spend some of my years for others. But, the time to pass the torch to someone else has come.

One of the precious points that I retain is: "To educate a kid you need the whole village, from his parents to the seller of breads".

Coding

Last year, I had my own physical business which required a website. I had to generate one with WordPress. It was nice but I did not like to be restricted in terms of creativity.

I went through Html CSS and Javascript by attaching a particular focus on their bases. I did not want to rush into libraries. I do not regret my choice. Later on, I learned the basics of React, Redux, Sass, Express, Node.js, and MongoDB. I keep learning as it never ends.

I would like to become a full-stack developer. I want to be able to create an application on mobile and a website from 0 to 100.

My view

Two houses could have the same outlook but may have different build-up which makes a huge difference.

In web development, I follow the same principle. I try to build my application properly. Otherwise, if I want to repair the roof I will end-up adjusting the floor.

This is why I focus my attention to write clean code or at least to aim toward it. After this, my pleasure is to make the website elegant.

I have no pretensions of having a clean code on all of my apps. But, I try to make it at least not messy. And, why not considered one day as a clean code from those who are well experienced.
